Welcome to GWC Katy!

Girls Who Code Katy is a youth-led chapter based in Obra D. Tompkins High School committed to engaging minorities in the world of computer science. By exposing high school girls to coding through collaborative projects, competitions, workshops, and guest speaker events, GWC Katy hopes to empower females to pursue CS throughout high school, college, and beyond.

Feel free to contact us anytime at gwc.katy@gmail.com.
